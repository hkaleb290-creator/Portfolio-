How to Play
Objective: Score the most points by playing strategic cards. Click cards in your hand to play them to the board. Match suits for bonus points. Build strategic sequences. First to reach 5 board cards wins the round.

Controls: Click a card in your hand to play it • Click "Draw Card" to skip and draw • Try to outscore your previous best!
About This Game
Zanzibar is a strategic card game developed collaboratively by Isaiah Jimenez and Kaleb Harris. This implementation features a full 52-card deck, score tracking, and game state management. Part of the portfolio showcasing game development and team collaboration skills.
